Just a Regular Gal

Jennifer Rae is a devoted mother and faithful child to God.

She has seen the good in life, but also recognized that good is not always God.

As a single mother from the age of 19 years old, she has persevered in the face

of adversity. Raising her children through attending university, career growth and

eventually surviving the horrors of homelessness in the most severe conditions.

She was educated at Syracuse University with Honors, but she is rooted in her

knowledge of truth, which comes from The Word of God and the still small voice inside.

Every story that comes to life on these pages has been lived by the woman you see here.

Today she writes to testify that with God's Grace and idominable faith miracles abound.

She stands as proof that God's seeds will flourish no matter the conditions.
