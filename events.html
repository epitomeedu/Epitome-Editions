Epitome Events

We are organizing a huge donation effort in collaboration with

A SafePlace of The Permian Basin. We are beyond grateful to God

to be able to give back to other mother's affected by domestic

abuse. We will never forget where we came from and we will never

abandon mothers when they need help most. We are dedicated to

making a difference in the lives of homeless mothers in West Texas

and eventually state and nation wide. We are also partnering with

Epitome Education, who has gratiously offered child care services to

mothers during their career search. We are committed to offering

these familes the support and services they so despertely need.

If you are interested in making a donation or offering support

please contact Jennifer Rae@epitomeEducation.org or (432)416-4556.

Event will take place on February 14, 2026

All donations need to be collected by February 10, 2026
